Guided tour to the Tränenpalast

In the time of the Berlin Wall, the Tränenpalast (Palace of Tears) at the Berlin Friedrichstraße station was a border crossing point between West and East Berlin. Nowadays, it's an interactive museum with an exhibition about the Cold War and the German reunification. More information can be found in www.hdg.de/en/traenenpalast/permanent-exhibition/.

Attending the guided tour

Please let us know by Monday, February 4th, if you would like to attend the tour: just write an email to bmsstudconf@gmail.com. While this is non-bindung, it is of tremendous help for our planning. We will use the resonance to gauge the interest and book the tour capacity accordingly; it is thus in your best interest to sign up. Entrance to the exhibition and the tour are free.

Wine and Cheese

The Wine and Cheese is an informal event where applicants, Berlin students and other conference participants can socialize in a relaxed environment. As its name indicates, this social event is accompanied by cheese and wine that has been selected by the organizers for the event. Nevertheless, there will be non-alcoholic options for those conference participants who don't drink alcohol.

The Wine and Cheese will take place at the BMS Lounge in the Technische Universitát Berlin. More information about its location can be found in the venue page. More information about the schedule will be available on the schedule page soon.

Every conference participant is invited to join the Wine and Cheese.
