Schedule

The talks of the conference will take place at the lecture hall MA 042, the Wine and Cheese event will be at the BMS Lounge (MA 209). Both are at the mathematics building of the Technische Universität Berlin, at Str. des 17. Juni 136. More information about the location can be found on the venue page. There will be an introduction to the conference on the first day of the conference fifteen minutes before the first talk.
