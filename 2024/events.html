Wine and Cheese

Our traditional Wine & Cheese evening is one of the highlights of the BMS year. It's an opportunity for all conference participants (including many current and prospective BMS students!) to socialise in a relaxed environment. As its name indicates, this social event is accompanied by different kinds of cheese and wine that have been selected by the organizers for the event. For those who don't drink alcohol or don't like cheese, there will be non-alcoholic drinks and some other snacks as well.

The Wine and Cheese will take place at the lecture hall H1035 in the Main Building of TU Berlin, starting at 5 pm. More information about its location can be found on the venue page. More information about the schedule can be found on the schedule page.

Every conference participant is invited to join the Wine and Cheese!
