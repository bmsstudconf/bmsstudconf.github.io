Prof. Dr. Marita Thomas

Freie Universität Berlin

Marita Thomas is Professor for Applied Analysis at Freie Universität Berlin since 2022. She is the head of the research group Bulk-Interface Processes at the Weierstrass Institute. Her scientific interest lies in material modeling, damage and fracture mechanics, coupling of optoelectronics with thermomechanics, and variational methods, Gamma-convergence, functions of bounded variation.

Thomas holds a PhD from Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin.

More information can be found on her webpage at WIAS Berlin or at her personal webpage.
