Registration

There is no registration fee. To register for attending the conference, including the social events but excluding the Career Event, please complete the registration form. The deadline is 12/02/2025. We need you to register for organizational purposes and to print your name tag, so speakers also have to fill out the form.

In case you would not like to use Google Forms, contact us at bmsstudconf@gmail.com; we will then inform you about the specific information that we need from you.

All attendants who wish to participate in the Career Event must register separately by filling out another form. The deadline is 12/02/2025 too. You can also register for the Career Event without attending the conference beforehand.
