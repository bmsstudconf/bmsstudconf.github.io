Venue

Warning! BVG strike on 20-21 February. Those days there is no U-Bahn, tram, most buses are cancelled. The S-Bahn, DB, Jelbi operates. Plan ahead!

Lecture hall H0111 (Talks, Career Event)

The talks of 13th BMS Student Conference will take place at the main building of the Technische Universität Berlin, which is located at Str. des 17. Juni 135, 10623 Berlin. All talks will take place in lecture hall H0111. To get there, please enter the building from the main entrance (i.e. from the side of the street "Straße des 17. Juni 135, 10623 Berlin") and just go to the right.

Lecture hall H1035 (Wine and Cheese)

The Wine and Cheese event will take place in the lecture hall H1035, at the Main building of the Technische Universität Berlin, which is located at Str. des 17. Juni 135, 10623 Berlin. To get there, please enter the building from the main entrance (i.e. from the side of the street "Straße des 17. Juni 135, 10623 Berlin") and take the stairs on the right to the first floor and follow the signs or ask anyone.

Tante Lisbeth (Kegeln)

We will play nine-pin bowling on Wednesday in the bar Tante Lisbeth, which is located under Muskauer Straße 49, 10997 Berlin. We will all go together from TU Berlin to the event location. Note that registration is necessary for this event.

Where to eat?

There are several places to eat near the conference venue. The first two in the list below offer special prices for students. The first two require payment by Mensa card; you can obtain one from the machines in front of the Mensa; for that, you need to pay a deposit of 1,55€, which will be refunded to you if you return your card before leaving Berlin. You will need to present a valid student ID to receive a discount on the Mensa prices. In the following list, the prices pertain to a simple menu excluding drinks. Student prices are used where applicable.

  • TU Mensa (3–7€; Mensa card only), Hardenbergstrasse 34.
  • TU Personnel Canteen (in German; 6–8€; cash only), in the ground floor of the new electrical engineering / computer science (EN) building, at the back side, Google maps
  • TU Math Cafeteria (4.50–6.50€; cash only), ground floor of the math building, serves indian food
  • Café Nero (4–8€; cash only), Volkswagenbibliothek, Fasanenstraße 88
  • Comprehensive listing of places

How to get there?

The venue is easy to reach by public transit, but due to the BVG strike only options 3 and 4 below are available on 20-21 February. Also consider using bikes, scooters, and sharing options; most of these are available in Jelbi.

  • U-Bahn line U2, at the stop U Ernst-Reuter Platz, plus approximately 5 minutes of walking.
  • Buses M45, X9 and 245, at the stop U Ernst-Reuter Platz or Marchstraße, plus approximately between 5 and 10 minutes of walking.
  • S-Bahn lines S3, S5, S7, and S9 at the stop S Tiergarten, plus approximately 10 minutes of walking.
  • U-Bahn lines U2, U9 or S-Bahn lines S3, S5, S7, S9 or regional trains at the stop S/U Zoologischer Garten, plus approximately 20 minutes of walking.

Route planning as well as information about the strike, further cancellations, and construction work within Berlin public transportation can be found online at fahrinfo.bvg.de.
